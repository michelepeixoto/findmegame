About

Find Me was created as a submission for the 10th Global Game Jam, held at the Bellevue College in January 2018. It was made to fit the competition's theme of transmission, in the way that you are transmitted texts as clues. Due to the short time given to complete it, the game was launched with several bugs. However our team is still currently working to fix them, so make sure to check back often!
